Pumpkin Pie Espresso Martinis

Ingredients

  • Double Shot of Espresso
  • 1 Tablespoon Brown Sugar (Swerve)
  • 4 Shots of Vodka
  • 1.5 Shots of Pumpkin Creamer

Instructions

  1. First, set your glasses in the freezer to chill.
  2. Brew your double espresso pod.
  3. Next, stir in the brown sugar to the hot espresso to mix well.
  4. Add ice to your shaker followed by all your ingredients.
  5. Shake well!
  6. Pour into your two martini glasses, top with pumpkin pie spice and enjoy!
